About


Hi, my name is Laurice Howard and I am a Junior Web Developer currently studying at ElevenFifty Academy. I am passionate about coming up with smart solution to solve complex problems and doing what's necessary to become better in my craft.

I studied Soicology at Purdue and after graduating in 2014 I briefly worked in the mortgage industry as a Mortgage Broker before moving on to work 5 years as a bartender eventually becoming a senior bartender at Metro NightCub and pursuing my passion in music. I've always loved technology and knew I wanted to pursue some sort of technical career aside from music, especially with all of the influences from my peers I had at Purdue. This last year that I've spent seriously building up my home studio in quarantine, getting some tunes recorded and doing more research in the technical world of music led me to want to learn how to build websites so I could help myself, my friends, and have a career skill outside of music that I am passionate about.
